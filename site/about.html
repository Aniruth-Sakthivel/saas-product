Our mission

HotelOS was founded by hoteliers and engineers who were tired of clunky, dated property management systems. We set out to build software as warm, polished, and effortless as a great stay.

Today we power hundreds of properties across the world — from boutique inns to multi-property resort groups — helping them fill more rooms, delight more guests, and spend less time on busywork.
